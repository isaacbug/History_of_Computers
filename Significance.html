  • The Bombe

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎It's a very complex decoding machine that Alan Turing and his team created to decode the Enigma machine during WW2.


  • Turing Test

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ It's a popular test named after Alan (Turing) that is used to test AI in order to see if it deserves its title as an intelligent machine as it tests the machines "human like thinking" nature.


Contribution

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ Often considered the father of modern computer science, Alan Turing was famous for his work developing the first modern computers, decoding the encryption of German Enigma machines during the second world war, and detailing a procedure known as the Turing Test, forming the basis for artificial intelligence.


Result

‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ Alan's machine, The Bombe, was able to decode the germans message sending, “Enigma” device granting access to their communication, which turned the tides for them to win the war. It is speculated that this breakthrough shortened the war by two years and saved around 14 million innocent lives.
