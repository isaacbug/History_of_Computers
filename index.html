History of Computers

Alan Turing

Father of Theoretical Computer Science



Who Was He?


  • Full Name: Alan Mathison Turing


  • Lived: June 23, 1912 - June 7, 1954


  • Life: Born in London and raised in Guildford, passed in Wilmslow

Parents

His dad was named Julius Mathison Turing and his mother was called Ethel Sarah Stoney


Education

Alan Turing went to Sherborne School where he had a great friendship with Christopher Morcom who sadly passed away from tuberculosis. He graduated from King's College, Cambridge, and in 1938, earned a doctorate degree from Princeton University.
